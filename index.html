What this is

Ability is rarely the limiting factor; access to information is.

Thousands of students finish at the top of their class every year with everything it takes to get into the world's leading universities. Many of them never apply, not out of indifference, but because the process is genuinely opaque without a network that has been through it. Ascensia is the attempt to close that gap by making the system legible to the people it was not designed to serve.

The program is free. The curriculum, the mentorship, the network, and the tools are all included. What we ask for in return is seriousness of purpose and the willingness to give back when you get through.

The Fellowship

Our four pillars

i. Curriculum

Nine tracks, built for international students.

Bi-weekly sessions covering standardized tests, essays, financial aid, college lists, activities, application platforms, and more. Instructors are students from a broad range of top global universities and backgrounds who have been through exactly this.

See the program
ii. Mentorship

One mentor. One fellow.

Each Fellow is paired one-to-one with a mentor currently enrolled at one of their target universities. Through bi-weekly sessions, essay feedback, extracurricular strategy, and support for mental well-being throughout the application journey, Fellows gain the kind of candid guidance that only someone who completed the process a cycle or two earlier can provide.

iii. Advisors network

If your target school is on the map, someone there is in our network.

Our advisors network spans 150+ universities across every major region. If you are building a college list, there is likely someone at each of those schools who applied from a background not unlike yours and is willing to talk.

Explore the network
iv. Counselle AI

Your admission companion, all in one place.

Fellows get full access to Counselle, Ascensia's AI-powered admissions platform. It pulls directly from official university Common Data Sets, financial aid records, and anonymized profiles of admitted students in the network. Deadline tracking, essay tools, university fit analysis, and communication with network students are all built in. There is nothing else like it available for free.

Who this is for

Students who are ready, regardless of where they started.

Ascensia is not a scholarship for the underprivileged, and it is not a recruiting pipeline for elite universities. It is a program for students who have what it takes to compete for the world's top institutions and who would benefit from having the right information, the right network, and the right guidance at the right time. We are looking for the next generation of changemakers and global citizens, wherever they come from.
