The program

One system.
Nothing left out.

A 14-month cohort program — curriculum, matched mentorship, application support, and a permanent alumni network. Free to every admitted student.

01

Curriculum

Nine tracks covering every component of global admissions — built for international students, not adapted from something else.

02

Mentorship

Each student is matched 1:1 with someone currently enrolled at one of their target universities. Someone who just did what you're trying to do.

03

Application Support

Hands-on help through every submission — essays, financial aid forms, activity lists, recommendation strategy, and deadlines.

04

Community

Students move through as a cohort. Every graduate joins the alumni mentor network permanently. The program gets stronger with each year.
