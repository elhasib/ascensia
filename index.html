The program

One complete
system. Nothing
left out.

Ascensia is a 14–16 month cohort-based program that takes high-potential students from the start of junior year through final application submission. Every component is designed to work together. The program is grant-funded and free to every student.

01

Curriculum

17 tracks covering every dimension of the application — testing, essays, financial aid, college research, non-US admissions, and more. Built for every global school system.

02

Mentorship

1:1 matching with a current student at one of the applicant's target universities. No textbook or consultant can replicate someone who just walked through the same door.

03

Application Support

Hands-on guidance through every component — essays, activity descriptions, recommendation strategy, financial aid forms, and deadline management across all platforms.

04

Community

The cohort model creates shared accountability and a network that persists for life. Every graduate joins the alumni mentor network — making the program stronger with every class.
