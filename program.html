The Program

9 tracks. Everything you need.

A free mentorship and curriculum program built specifically for international students from underserved regions. Structured, personal, and built to last.

What it is

One system.
Nothing left out.

Ascensia is a 14-month cohort program built from scratch for international students from underrepresented regions. A 9-track curriculum, matched 1:1 mentorship, hands-on application support, and a cohort community — all working together, at no cost to the student.

Admissions are need-blind. No student is turned away for financial reasons. The pilot cohort will onboard 20 students from Egypt and South Africa, matched with 20 mentors from top universities globally.

Any student with the ambition and academic foundation to compete for top global universities is eligible.

01

Curriculum

9 tracks covering every component of global admissions. Built for international students — not adapted from US domestic programs. Essays, testing, financial aid, activities, and non-US systems.

02

Mentorship

Matched 1:1 with someone currently enrolled at one of their target universities, by school, region, and field. Not historical advice — someone who just went through the same process.

03

Application Support

Hands-on help through every submission: essays, financial aid forms, activity lists, recommendation strategy, and every deadline. Active and specific at every step.

04

Community

Students move through the program as a cohort. Every graduate enters the alumni mentor network permanently. Each cohort makes the next one stronger.

What we teach

9 tracks.
Built from scratch.

Every track was designed for international students from underserved backgrounds. Nothing here is adapted from a US domestic program.

T1

Standardized Tests

SAT, ACT, AP, IB, A-Level, and all international equivalents. Strategy, preparation, and how admissions offices actually use scores.

T2

Academic Courses

Transcripts, GPA, course rigor, and academic positioning across every major school system globally.

T3

Essays and Writing

Personal statement, all supplement types, UCAS personal statement. Finding and communicating a genuine story.

T4

Activities

Building, describing, and presenting an activity profile with clarity and precision across every application platform.

T5

Honors and Awards

Competitions, olympiads, recognition programs. How to find them, present them, and how admissions offices evaluate them.

T6

Application Platforms

Common App, QuestBridge, UCAS, Coalition, and all major platforms. Every section, every deadline, every submission.

T7

College List Strategy

Building a balanced, financially viable list that matches ambition with strategy. Understanding fit, selectivity, and aid policies.

T8

Non-US Admissions

UK, Canada, Europe, Australia, and other systems. Complete coverage for students building a global college list.

T9

Need-Based Financial Aid

Need-blind vs need-aware, CSS Profile, appeals, aid comparison, and what full financial coverage actually means in practice.

Who we serve

Need-blind admissions.
Open to all.

Ascensia does not discriminate on the basis of geography, school type, or background. Any student with the ambition and academic foundation to compete for top global universities is eligible to apply.

Express interest

Any grade, any school

Students in Grades 10 and 11 are prioritized. Grade 12 students with exceptional profiles may also be considered on a case-by-case basis.

Any background

Ascensia was built for students who lack access to counseling, mentorship, and support. There are no geographic restrictions on eligibility.

Need-blind admissions

Full need-based and merit-based financial aid is available. No student is turned away due to financial circumstances — ever.

Selection philosophy

We select students who, with the program's support, can become competitive applicants — not only those who already are. Potential is the primary criterion.
