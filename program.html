The Program

9 tracks. Everything you need.

A free mentorship and curriculum program built for international students from underserved regions. Structured, personal, and built to last.

What it is

One system.
Nothing left out.

Ascensia is a 14-month cohort program built from scratch for international students from underrepresented regions. A 9-track curriculum, matched 1:1 mentorship, hands-on application support, and a cohort community — all working together, at no cost to the student.

Admissions are need-blind. The pilot cohort will onboard 20 students from Egypt and South Africa, matched with 20 mentors from top universities globally.

The journey

Structured around
the student's year.

Phase 1

Foundation

Students are onboarded, matched with their mentor, and introduced to the full curriculum. College list strategy, financial aid orientation, and standardized testing planning all happen here.

Orientation · College list · Testing
Phase 2

Profile Building

Activities, academic positioning, honors, and extracurricular documentation are shaped and refined. The mentor relationship is in full operation with regular structured sessions.

Mentor sessions · Activities · Positioning
Phase 3

Writing and Submission

Personal statement development, supplement essays, financial aid form completion, recommendation strategy, and final application submission across all platforms.

Essays · Financial aid · Submission
Phase 4

Decisions and Transition

Financial aid comparison, appeals support, and decision guidance. Students transition into the alumni mentor network, trained to guide the next cohort through the same process.

Decisions · Appeals · Alumni network

What we teach

9 tracks.
Built from scratch.

Every track was designed for international students from underserved backgrounds. Nothing here is adapted from a US domestic program.

T1

Standardized Tests

SAT, ACT, AP, IB, A-Level, and international equivalents. Strategy, preparation, and how scores are actually read.

T2

Academic Courses

Transcripts, GPA, course rigor, and academic positioning across every major school system globally.

T3

Essays and Writing

Personal statement, all supplement types, UCAS personal statement. Finding and communicating a genuine story.

T4

Activities

Building, describing, and presenting an activity profile with clarity and precision across every platform.

T5

Honors and Awards

Competitions, olympiads, recognition programs. How to find them, present them, and how they're evaluated.

T6

Application Platforms

Common App, QuestBridge, UCAS, Coalition, and all major platforms. Every section, every deadline.

T7

College List

Building a balanced, financially viable list that matches ambition with strategy. Fit, selectivity, and aid policies.

T8

Non-US Admissions

UK, Canada, Europe, Australia, and other systems. Complete coverage for students building a global list.

T9

Financial Aid

Need-blind vs need-aware, CSS Profile, appeals, aid comparison. What full coverage actually means in practice.

Who we serve

Need-blind.
Open to all.

Any student with the ambition and academic foundation to compete for top global universities is eligible to apply. No student is turned away for financial reasons.

Any grade, any school

Students in Grades 10 and 11 are prioritized. Grade 12 students with strong profiles may be considered on a case-by-case basis.

Any background

Ascensia was built for students who lack access to counseling and mentorship. There are no geographic restrictions on eligibility.

Need-blind admissions

Full need-based and merit-based financial aid is available. No student is turned away due to financial circumstances.

Potential first

We select students who, with the program's support, can become competitive applicants — not only those who already are.
