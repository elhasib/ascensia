The Program

9 tracks. Everything you need.

A free mentorship and curriculum program for students who are ready for the world's top universities, regardless of where they are coming from or how much support they have had until now.

What it is

One system.
Nothing left out.

Ascensia is a 14-month cohort program built specifically for international students from underrepresented regions. A 9-track curriculum, matched 1:1 mentorship, hands-on application support, access to the Counselle platform, and a cohort community — all working together, at no cost to the student.

Admissions are need-blind. The pilot cohort will onboard students from Egypt and South Africa, matched with mentors from top universities globally. Grade 10 students are prioritized, though Grade 11 students with strong profiles are also considered.

The journey

Structured around
the student's year.

Phase 1

Foundation

Students are onboarded, matched with their mentor, and introduced to the full curriculum. College list strategy, financial aid orientation, and standardized testing planning all happen here.

Orientation · College list · Testing
Phase 2

Profile Building

Activities, academic positioning, honors, and extracurricular documentation are shaped and refined. The mentor relationship is in full operation with regular structured sessions.

Mentor sessions · Activities · Positioning
Phase 3

Writing and Submission

Personal statement development, supplement essays, financial aid form completion, recommendation strategy, and final application submission across all platforms.

Essays · Financial aid · Submission
Phase 4

Decisions and Transition

Financial aid comparison, appeals support, and decision guidance. Students transition into the alumni mentor network, where they become the people guiding the next cohort through the same process.

Decisions · Appeals · Alumni network

What we teach

9 tracks.
Built from scratch.

Every track was designed for international students. None of this is adapted from a US domestic program, because that is not who this is for.

T1

Standardized Tests

SAT, ACT, AP, IB, A-Level, and international equivalents. Strategy, preparation, and how scores are actually read by admissions offices.

T2

Academic Courses

Transcripts, GPA, course rigor, and academic positioning across every major school system globally.

T3

Essays and Writing

Personal statement, all supplement types, UCAS personal statement. Finding and communicating a genuine story without losing yourself in the process.

T4

Activities

Building, describing, and presenting an activity profile with clarity and precision across every application platform.

T5

Honors and Awards

Competitions, olympiads, recognition programs. How to find them, present them, and how admissions offices evaluate them.

T6

Application Platforms

Common App, QuestBridge, UCAS, Coalition, and all major platforms. Every section, every deadline, no surprises.

T7

College List

Building a balanced, financially viable list that matches ambition with strategy. Fit, selectivity, and aid policies for international students.

T8

Non-US Admissions

UK, Canada, Europe, Australia, and other systems. Complete coverage for students building a genuinely global list.

T9

Financial Aid

Need-blind vs need-aware, CSS Profile, appeals, aid comparison. What full coverage actually means and how to get it.

Included with the fellowship

Counselle, our admissions platform.

Fellows get full access to Counselle, an AI-powered admissions platform built specifically for this process. It pulls from official university Common Data Sets, financial aid records, and anonymized admitted-student profiles from within the Ascensia network. Everything you need in one place, and none of it available anywhere else for free.

What's inside Counselle

University data
Common Data Sets and financial aid records for hundreds of universities, updated continuously.
Admitted profiles
Anonymized profiles of students in the network who were accepted, searchable by school and background.
Deadlines dashboard
Every deadline, every requirement, tracked in one place across your entire college list.
Network communication
Direct access to students at your target universities through the Ascensia advisor network.
Generative AI tools
Essay drafting, activity description writing, financial aid letter review — purpose-built for the admissions process.
Fit analysis
What each university is actually looking for in international students, drawn from official sources, not guesswork.

Who we serve

Need-blind.
Open to all.

Any student with the ambition and academic foundation to compete for top global universities is eligible to apply. We are looking for the next generation of global leaders and changemakers, wherever they are coming from. No student is turned away for financial reasons.

Grade 10 prioritized

The program is designed as a multi-phase preparation journey. Grade 11 students with strong profiles may also be considered on a case-by-case basis.

Any background

Ascensia was built for students who lack access to counseling and mentorship. There are no geographic restrictions on eligibility.

Need-blind admissions

Full need-based and merit-based aid is available. The students who most need this program are often the last ones to find it, and that asymmetry is the reason we exist.

Potential first

We select students who, with the program's support, can become genuinely competitive applicants — not only those who already are. That distinction matters.
