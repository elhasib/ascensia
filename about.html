About Ascensia

Built by people who lived the gap.

Every person on the Ascensia team has a personal connection to the problem — either they lived it themselves, or they watched someone they loved lose to it. That's not a marketing line. It's why the organization exists.

2025
Our story

The program
we wish had
existed.

Ascensia started with a simple observation: the students who most need access to world-class universities are the ones least likely to know those universities are financially within reach. The Harvard financial aid program. The eight need-blind schools. The 60% of international students who receive aid. None of this is secret — it's just never been put in front of the right people.

The founders weren't admissions professionals or academics. They were students from underrepresented regions who found their way through — by accident, through a chance encounter, through stubborn research at midnight — and realized that the distance between "I couldn't have done this" and "I did this" was mostly information. And one person who had already done it.

That's what Ascensia is. The information, and the person. Structured as a program. Free to every student. Designed to compound: every cohort produces mentors for the next.

What we stand for

Four principles.
No exceptions.

E

Excellence

We hold our curriculum, mentors, and students to the highest standard — not as gatekeeping, but as respect. These students are competing for the best institutions in the world and we treat them accordingly.

A

Access

The students who need this most are often the ones least likely to find it. That asymmetry is the entire reason Ascensia exists. Every decision — funding model, program design, outreach — runs through this lens.

A

Authenticity

We help students find and amplify their real story — never a manufactured persona. The best applications are honest. We believe that because it's true and because it's the right thing to believe.

C

Community

Every student who goes through Ascensia becomes part of something bigger than their own application. The community is how the program gets stronger over time — and how the next student's path is shorter than the last one's.

The team

People first.
Everything else follows.

The Ascensia team is recruited, not assembled. Every person here has a reason that isn't a job title — a student they lost to the gap, a door they opened for themselves and want to hold open for someone else. This is that team.
