About Ascensia

Built by people who lived the gap.

Every member of the team either navigated international admissions from an underserved region, or has spent their career working to close the gap that defines it.

Our story

Why we built this.

Ascensia started with a simple observation: universities in the United States, Canada, and Europe have billions of dollars in financial aid reserved for students from exactly the regions we come from. Most of those students never apply — because they have no idea the money exists, no one to guide them through the process, and no model of what success looks like.

The members of this team applied to top universities from Egypt, Kenya, and Venezuela. We know what that process looks like without a counselor, without money for consultants, without a single person in your school who has done it before. We built Ascensia because we spent years wishing it existed.

The program is free. It will always be free. The problem is not that students lack motivation — it is that they lack access to the infrastructure that makes the process legible. That is what we are building.

What we believe

Four principles
that shape everything.

A
Access

Admission should reflect talent, effort, and potential — not geography, income, or luck of network. We exist to make that more true.

R
Rigor

We hold our students to a high standard, because the universities we prepare them for do the same. Warmth and rigor are not in conflict.

H
Honesty

We do not overpromise. We tell students what they need to hear — about their application, their chances, and what still needs work.

C
Community

Every student who goes through Ascensia becomes part of something permanent. The program gets better because people give back.
