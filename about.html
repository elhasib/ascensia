Our story

We are the
people we
have been
waiting for.

Even as students at institutions like STEM High School and the African Leadership Academy, our team saw the scale of what was missing. Participation in selective global programs gave us access to international networks that transformed our trajectories. It also made visible the millions of talented peers left behind without any comparable guidance or mentorship.

Some of us were able to connect with peers at top institutions through selective program networks, but only within those immediate circles. Beyond such programs, there is effectively no pathway for most students navigating this process. Ascensia is built to close that gap through structured, matched mentorship rather than leaving it to chance.

Our primary research, conducted in March 2026 across 77 student respondents in Egypt and Nigeria, confirmed what we already knew from experience. The demand is real. 87% of respondents expressed strong interest in the Ascensia model. The infrastructure to meet that demand does not currently exist at any meaningful scale.

What we stand for

Four principles.
No exceptions.

E

Excellence

We hold our curriculum, mentors, and students to a high standard. Not as gatekeeping, but as respect. Students applying to the world's most selective universities deserve preparation that matches that ambition.

A

Access

Admissions are need-blind. Full need-based and merit-based financial aid is available. The students who most need this program are often the ones least likely to find it. That asymmetry is the reason Ascensia exists.

A

Authenticity

We help students find and present their genuine story. The best applications are honest. We build the skills to communicate that honestly and compellingly across any application system in the world.

C

Community

Every student who completes Ascensia enters the alumni mentor network permanently. The program compounds with every cohort. Each class makes the next one stronger, more informed, and better supported.
