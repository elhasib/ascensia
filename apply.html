Common questions

Things people
usually ask.

Is the program really free?

Yes, entirely. The program is grant-funded and partnership-funded. No student is ever turned away for financial reasons — that principle is structural, not aspirational.

What grades / years are eligible?

Students currently in Grade 10 or Grade 11 (Year 11 or Year 12 in UK systems). We accept students across all school types — government, private national, and international schools.

What countries are eligible?

Ascensia currently targets Africa, South Asia, Latin America, and the Middle East. If you're in another region and believe you fit the profile, apply — we read every application individually.

How much time does the program take?

The program runs 14–16 months from orientation through application submission. Expect approximately 3–5 hours per week during the school year, with heavier periods around essay season and application deadlines.

What if my grades aren't perfect?

We select for potential, not perfection. We want students who, with Ascensia's support, can become competitive — not students who are already fully prepared. If you have drive and ambition, apply.

When will I hear back?

Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis. You will receive a response within three weeks of submission. If you are invited to continue, the next step is a short interview with a member of the Ascensia team.
