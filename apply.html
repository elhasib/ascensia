Common questions

Things people
usually ask.

When will applications open?

Ascensia is finalizing its pilot cohort and is not yet accepting public applications. To be notified when we open, send an email to team@ascensia.foundation.

Who is eligible to apply?

Any high-potential student from any country who aspires to attend a top global university is eligible. Ascensia does not discriminate by geography, school type, or financial background. Admissions are need-blind.

What grades are prioritized?

Students in Grades 10 and 11 are prioritized — the program is designed as a multi-phase preparation journey. Grade 12 students with exceptional profiles may also be considered.

Is the program free?

Ascensia is free to all admitted students. No student is ever turned away for financial reasons. Full need-based and merit-based aid is available.

What does the pilot cohort look like?

The first cohort will onboard 20 students from Egypt and South Africa, matched with 20 mentors from top universities globally, and run the full 9-track curriculum via digital platforms.

Where can I learn more?

Visit the Program page for a full overview of the curriculum, mentorship model, and timeline. For anything else, reach the team at team@ascensia.foundation.
