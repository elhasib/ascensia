Common questions

Things people
usually ask.

When will applications open?

Ascensia is in the process of finalizing its pilot cohort and is not yet accepting public applications. To be notified when we open, send an email to team@ascensia.foundation.

Who is eligible to apply?

Ascensia does not discriminate on the basis of geography, school type, or background. Any high-potential student who aspires to attend a top global university is eligible. Admissions are need-blind.

What grades are prioritized?

Students in Grades 10 and 11 are prioritized, as the program is designed as a multi-phase preparation journey. Grade 12 students with exceptional profiles may be considered during the pilot phase.

Is the program free?

Ascensia offers full need-based and merit-based financial aid. Admissions are need-blind, meaning ability to pay is not a factor in the selection decision. No student is turned away due to financial circumstances.

What does the pilot cohort look like?

The first cohort will onboard 20 students from Egypt and South Africa, matched with 20 mentors from top universities. The 9-track curriculum will be delivered via digital platforms with structured feedback built in from day one.

Where can I learn more?

Visit the Program page for a full overview of the curriculum, mentorship model, and roadmap. For any other questions, contact the team at team@ascensia.foundation.
