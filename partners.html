For Partners & Funders

A model built to scale with capital.

Every dollar invested in Ascensia produces a measurable, verifiable result — a student placed at a top university who could not otherwise have gotten there. The cost per outcome is among the most efficient in global education.

Why partner

The return
is permanent.

Ascensia's funding model is designed for partnerships with foundations, universities, corporations, and governments. The program doesn't create dependency — it creates multipliers. Every student who graduates becomes a mentor, making the next cohort cheaper to run and more effective to deliver.

Intelligence and talent are evenly distributed across the world. The students Ascensia serves have already proven they belong in the conversation. The gap isn't ability — it's information, access, and one person who has already walked through the door.

Philanthropic Foundations

Mastercard Foundation, Gates Foundation, Aga Khan Foundation, and peers committed to expanding access to elite higher education for underrepresented global students. Ascensia offers a direct, measurable channel for that mission.

Universities

Institutions seeking genuinely diverse, globally representative incoming classes. Ascensia delivers prepared, informed international applicants from underrepresented regions — students who would never have applied without the program.

Corporate Partners

Companies building talent pipelines from underrepresented regions. Today's Ascensia student is tomorrow's most promising hire. A long-term investment in human capital with traceable, verifiable returns.

Governments

Ministries of education and national development agencies placing top national talent at global institutions as a component of long-term human capital strategy. Ascensia provides infrastructure for that pipeline.

The data that matters

Cost to every student

No student is ever turned away for financial reasons. The program is funded entirely through grants and partnerships — allowing selection on merit, not ability to pay.

8
Need-blind universities

The schools Ascensia prepares students for will cover 100% of demonstrated financial need for international students. A student from a family earning under $85,000 may pay nothing at Harvard. The gap is awareness, not money.

∞
The alumni multiplier

Every student who completes Ascensia becomes a mentor for the next cohort. The program compounds: each class makes the next one cheaper, more informed, and more effective to run. The return on early investment grows permanently.
